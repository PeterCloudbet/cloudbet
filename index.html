How To Use

1 Enter your Affiliate ID in the provided field.

2 Select the desired page from the dropdown menu.

3 Choose the language for the page from another dropdown menu.

4 Add 'Source' and 'Medium' parameters for more detailed tracking.



Additional Campaign Parameters

  • aftm_token= You can find your Affiliate Token on the Affiliate Portal
  • aftm_campaign= Give your name a campaign so you can track its performance 'FreeSpinsOffer'
  • aftm_medium= Your main traffic source 'social', 'email', 'organic'
  • aftm_source= Where within your medium are your sales coming from? 'facebook', 'newsletterA', 'website'
  • aftm_content= Where is your link nested? 'ImageA', 'TextB', 'Video1'


Enter your details and click the "Generate Link" button
