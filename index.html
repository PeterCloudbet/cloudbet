Generate Your Link

aftm_token=
This is your unique affiliate token. You can find it in the top corner of the Affiliate Portal.


Additional Campaign Parameters

aftm_campaign=
Name your campaign for tracking purposes, e.g., 'FreeSpinsOffer'.

aftm_medium=
Specify the medium, e.g., 'social', 'email', 'organic'.

aftm_source=
Identify the source, e.g., 'facebook', 'newsletterA'.

aftm_content=
Track content variations, e.g., 'ImageA', 'TextB'.
